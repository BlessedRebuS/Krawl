About Jane

Jane Smith is an award‑winning novelist known for her gripping thrillers and heartfelt literary fiction. With over ten novels published and translations in more than twenty languages, she has captivated readers worldwide. When she isn’t writing, Jane enjoys hiking, photography, and mentoring aspiring writers through workshops and online courses.
