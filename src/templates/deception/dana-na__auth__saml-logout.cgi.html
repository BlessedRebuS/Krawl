SAML Single Logout Service

This service handles SAML-based single logout requests for federated identity providers.

Note: This endpoint processes SAML 2.0 Single Logout requests. Ensure the SAMLLogoutRequest is properly deflated and base64-encoded as per the SAML 2.0 specification. Invalid or malformed requests will be logged for audit purposes.
