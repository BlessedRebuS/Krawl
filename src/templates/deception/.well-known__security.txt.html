Policy

We follow responsible disclosure guidelines and request that reporters:

  • Make a good faith effort to avoid privacy violations, destruction of data, and interruption or degradation of our service
  • Only interact with accounts you own or with explicit permission from the account holder
  • Not exploit a security issue for any purpose beyond demonstrating proof-of-concept
  • Give us reasonable time to remediate the issue before public disclosure

We accept reports in the following languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese.
