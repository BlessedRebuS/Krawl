 New Security Patch Released for Enterprise Suite https://www.acme-corp.com/blog/security-patch-june-2024 2024-06-13T09:15:00Z Critical security update addresses CVE-2024-31415 in Acme Enterprise Suite v4.2 and later.

June 13, 2024 — Acme Corp has released a critical security patch for its Enterprise Suite software to address a remotely exploitable vulnerability (CVE-2024-31415) that could allow unauthenticated attackers to execute arbitrary code.

The patch is available for immediate download via the Acme Update Center or through automatic update mechanisms. All customers running versions 4.2 through 4.5 are urged to apply the update within 72 hours.

No evidence of active exploitation has been detected in the wild, but the vulnerability is rated CVSS 9.8 (Critical).

Learn more: Security Advisory ACME-SA-2024-007

 Acme Corp Announces Partnership with GreenTech Solutions https://www.acme-corp.com/news/partnership-greentech 2024-06-12T16:00:00Z Strategic alliance to integrate renewable energy monitoring into Acme's IoT platform.

June 12, 2024 — Acme Corp today announced a strategic partnership with GreenTech Solutions to embed real-time renewable energy analytics into its Industrial IoT platform.

The integration will allow manufacturing clients to monitor solar, wind, and hydro energy consumption alongside operational metrics, enabling optimized energy efficiency and carbon footprint reduction.

The joint solution is expected to launch in Q3 2024 and will be available to existing Acme IoT customers at no additional cost.
