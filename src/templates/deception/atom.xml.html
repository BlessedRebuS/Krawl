SQL injection remains one of the most dangerous vulnerabilities. This article shows how to defend against it properly.

Example of bad code:

<?php
$query = "SELECT * FROM users WHERE username = '$_POST[username]' AND password = '$_POST[password]'";
?>

Example of good code:

<?php
