Policy

Scope: This policy applies to all digital assets owned by Example Corp, including websites, applications, and network infrastructure.

Safe Harbor: We promise not to pursue legal action against individuals who make a good faith effort to comply with this policy.

Preferred Languages: English, Spanish, French, German.

Response Time: We aim to acknowledge reports within 24 hours and provide an initial assessment within 3 business days.

Disclosure: We support coordinated vulnerability disclosure and will work with researchers to resolve issues before public disclosure, typically within 90 days.

What We Consider In Scope

  • Web applications (examplecorp.com, subdomains)
  • Mobile applications (iOS/Android)
  • Network infrastructure and servers
  • APIs and web services
  • Cloud storage and services

What We Consider Out of Scope

  • Denial of Service (DoS/DDoS) testing
  • Social engineering of employees
  • Physical security testing
  • Third-party services not owned or operated by Example Corp
  • Vulnerabilities in outdated software versions no longer supported

Safe Testing Guidelines

Please avoid:

  • Accessing, modifying, or deleting user data
  • Attempting to access accounts without explicit permission
  • Using automated tools that may disrupt service (e.g., scanners that generate excessive traffic)
  • Exploiting vulnerabilities to gain unauthorized access to systems
  • Sharing vulnerability details publicly before we have had a chance to resolve them
